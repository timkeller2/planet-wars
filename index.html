How to Play

  • Left-click: Select planets or ships. Click empty space to deselect.
  • Left-click + Drag: Draw a lasso to box-select ships or planets. Hold Shift to add/remove from selection.
  • Right-click: Issue orders! Right click an enemy to attack, a friendly planet to reinforce, or empty space to move ships there.
  • Right-click Drag / Middle-click Drag: Pan the camera.
  • Scroll Wheel / +/- keys: Zoom in and out.
  • Arrow Keys: Pan the camera.
  • Touch: Tap to select. One-finger drag to pan. Pinch to zoom.
  • Enter: Open/send chat messages.
  • Pause/Break: Pause the game.
  • Speed Keys (1, 2, 3): Set fleet speed to 1/4, 1/2, or normal. Keys 2 and 3 also set the default speed for future launches.
  • W — Warp: Toggle warp mode for the next launch.
  • B — Bomb Eco: Launch eco bombers (requires 🚀 Military planet).
  • Q — Bomb Ships: Launch ship bombers (requires 🚀 Military planet).
  • F — Fill Mode: Send only enough ships to fill the target planet.
  • S — Scout Mode: Send a small scouting party (10% of garrison, min 3).
  • I — Interceptor: Launch fast interceptor ships (can be launched from any planet).
  • C — Cruiser: Build a cruiser from a 🚀 Military planet or your Homeworld.
  • L — Leaderboard: Toggle the leaderboard panel.
  • Elimination: Be the last player standing. All other players must lose every planet and ship.
  • Tech Domination: Achieve a √Tech bonus of at least 15% AND lead 2nd place by at least 10 percentage points.
  • Economic Domination: Control more than 75% of the galaxy's total planet capacity.
  • Player Elimination: Capturing a player's last planet eliminates them, awarding the conqueror +100 Exp.
  • Production: Owned planets auto-produce ships. Rate scales with how many ships the planet is missing, boosted by Tech Score. Human production above 1× is reduced by 1/3.
  • Low Population Penalty: Planets below 50 ships suffer reduced production. Below 5 ships, production drops to just 10%.
  • Capacity Growth: Full planets slowly gain +1 max capacity. Homeworlds gain +2.
  • Tech Score 🧪: Earned when planets sit at max capacity. Chance decreases as your economy grows. √Tech gives +1% per point to kill chance, speed, production, dogfighting, and lowers launch costs.
  • Exp Score 🎯: Earned through combat (every 20 kills = +1 Exp). Eliminating a player or destroying a Homeworld awards +100 Exp. √Exp × 0.5 provides combat bonuses.
  • Gravity Fields: Planets project gravity wells (radius = maxShips × 1.5, expanded by Tech/Exp). Friendly wells protect ships from attrition and allow cruiser healing/rearming. Gravity gives +1% kill bonus per 50 friendly capacity nearby, -1% per 50 enemy.
  • Orbital Bombardment: Each defending ship killed has an 8% chance to reduce max capacity by 1. Below 55 max capacity = planet destroyed!
  • Planet Explosion: Destroyed planets deal 3d6 splash damage to all planets within 400px (reduced by 1 per 25px). Max capacity takes 1/3 of that. Military planet explosions deal 3× damage in a 600px radius. Chain reactions are possible!
  • Sacrificial Upgrade: Sending ships to a full friendly planet sacrifices them. Every 20 sacrificed = +1 max capacity.
  • Capture Penalty: Capturing an owned planet costs -1 max capacity. Capturing a neutral has a 40% chance to assign a special type.
  • Homeworld 👑: Starting planet. 2× production, +2 capacity growth. Attacking or defending grants +15% combat bonus (human players only). Destroying one awards +100 Exp.
  • Special Planet Types (30% chance on neutral capture):
    • 🧪 Research (10%): Earns +2 Tech per tick instead of +1.
    • 🚀 Military (10%): Required to launch bomber runs and cruisers. Costs -1 max capacity per bombing launch.
    • ⚡ Speed (10%): Ships launched gain +15 flat speed bonus.
  • Launch Cost: Each launch costs 10 + your planet count − √Tech ships. Half the remaining garrison is sent. Invest in Tech to launch cheaply!
  • Combat (Planet Attack): 80% base kill chance, minus 1% per 5 defending ships, plus Tech/Exp/Gravity/Swarm modifiers. Minimum kill chance = attacker's Tech+Exp bonuses.
  • Swarm Bonus: +1% kill chance per 10 friendly ships within 100px of the target.
  • Homeworld & Last Stand: +15% combat bonus when attacking or defending a Homeworld (human only). +15% Last Stand bonus defending your last planet. These do not apply to AI vs AI.
  • Human vs Human: Defender gets +2% bonus per surviving AI player. Clear out the AI first!
  • Dogfighting: Ships within 40px (expanded by Tech + Health) fire lasers each second. Base hit chance: (10 + Tech + Exp + ShipExp + maxHealth×5 + bombs×3) / 100. Interceptors get +5% bonus.
  • Fleet Attrition: Every 4 seconds, one ship per fleet is destroyed (after a safe window based on Tech+Exp). Ships inside friendly gravity wells take 1/3 attrition.
  • Deep Space Attrition: Individual ships in deep space take escalating damage the longer they fly, starting after a safe window of (√Tech + √Exp×0.5) / 2 seconds. Bombers take half attrition.
  • Speed Modifiers (1/2/3): 1/4 speed gets a 90% Saving Roll vs damage. 1/2 speed gets 75%. Cruisers and Amoebas always survive rolls.
  • Warp Drive (W): Adds (30 − your planet count, min 5) speed bonus. Each ship has a (50% − √Tech)% chance to explode on launch!
  • Bombers (B/Q): Military 🚀 planets only. Sends 1/3 of fleet at speed 20. Costs -1 max capacity.
    • B — Eco Bomb: 1d2 damage to planet max capacity.
    • Q — Ship Bomb: 1d4 damage to planet garrison.
  • Fill Mode (F): Send only enough to fill the target planet to max capacity.
  • Scout Mode (S): Send 10% of garrison (min 3 ships).
  • Hazard Combat Penalty: Nebulae reduce kill chance by intensity/4 %. Ion Storms reduce by intensity/2 % (modified by knowledge/tech).
  • Cruisers 🛸: Capital ships built from Military planets or Homeworlds (requires 60+ ships and 60+ max capacity). Press C to build.
  • Cruiser Stats: Health = garrison/25. Cost = health×15 ships + health×2 max capacity. Speed decreases with health. They orbit enemy planets instead of landing.
  • Multi-shot: Cruisers fire multiple laser shots per second equal to their current health, targeting enemy ships and planets.
  • Bomb Sacs: Cruisers carry bombs equal to 1/5 of their max health. Each dogfight volley at planets consumes one bomb sac. Bombs reload at friendly gravity wells (1 per 5 seconds).
  • Damage Resistance: Cruisers have a (50% + maxHealth×3%) chance to shrug off hits entirely, up to 80%.
  • Healing: Cruisers heal slowly inside friendly gravity wells. Outside wells, they burn fuel; when fuel runs out, they take slow health decay and move at reduced speed.
  • Planet Scramble: AI and neutral planets with 30+ ships will auto-launch interceptors at nearby enemy cruisers every 15 seconds.
  • Interceptors 🔺: Fast ships (speed 40) with +5% dogfight bonus. Created by pressing I from a non-special planet (permanently marks it). Interceptors have 50% penalty attacking planets but excel at killing ships in space.
  • Interceptor Landing: Interceptors have a 30% chance to crash when landing on a friendly planet.
  • Ion Storms ⚡: Moving hazards that drift across the map. New storms spawn roughly every 5 minutes. Intensity = 10d6 (capped by spawn count). Ships and planets inside take periodic damage.
  • Ancient Minefields 💣: Stationary. Intensity = 12d6. Massive burst damage on entry, especially to fast ships. Ongoing damage to ships inside. Do not damage planets.
  • Nebulae 🌫️: Stationary, 30% larger. Intensity = 10d8. Slow ships passing through, reduce combat effectiveness by intensity/4 %. No direct damage or entry burst.
  • Damage Formula: Chance = (intensity − knowledge − (√Tech + √Exp)/2 − √ShipExp) / divisor. Higher stats = much safer.
  • Knowledge: Increases when your planet gravity wells overlap a hazard (+1 per planet per 2 minutes). Directly reduces effective intensity.
  • Speed & Entry: Ships traveling faster than base speed (warp) take 3× entry damage from storms/minefields. Slow ships get Saving Rolls.
  • Amoeba Immunity: Amoebas are immune to Ion Storm entry damage (but not minefields).
  • Planet Erosion: Ion storms (not minefields) erode planet max capacity over time. Below 55 = destroyed! Last Stand gives +20 knowledge bonus.
  • Visuals: Yellow ⚡ = Ion Storm. Blue 💣 = Minefield. Purple 🌫️ = Nebula.
  • Giant Space Amoebas 🧬: Alien creatures belonging to the "Monsters" faction. They wander the map attacking ships and bombarding planets.
  • Amoeba Hive: The Monsters' homeworld. Spawns a new Amoeba every 2 minutes. Initial Amoebas also spawn scattered across the map at game start (1 per 10 planets).
  • Stats: Speed = 4d6 (varies per Amoeba). Health = 1–3 (random). Attack range = 50px. They fire dark green projectiles with yellow borders.
  • Regeneration: Amoebas slowly heal over time (1 HP per minute). When reduced to 0 health, if maxHealth > 0, they lose 1 maxHealth but fully regenerate — shrinking visually each time.
  • Growth & Splitting: Killing an enemy ship or bombarding a planet grants +1 maxHealth and +1 health (30-second cooldown). If maxHealth exceeds 4, the Amoeba splits into two smaller Amoebas (each with 2 HP).
  • Bomb Sacs: Amoebas carry bomb sacs equal to their maxHealth. These regenerate naturally (1 per 60 seconds) without needing a friendly well.
  • Combat Value: Killing an Amoeba awards 10 × maxHealth Exp to the attacker — they're valuable targets!
  • Wandering AI: Amoebas roam randomly toward planets. They attack any non-Monster ships in range and bombard unowned or enemy planets.
  • AI Spawning: AIs spawn gradually over the first few minutes. Count is configurable at game start (default 7).
  • AI Aggression: AIs act more frequently as they grow (2s base interval, −200ms per planet, min 500ms). They prefer nearby weak targets.
  • Defense Thresholds: AIs with fewer than 3 planets won't attack unless at 75% garrison. Large planets (150+ cap) are held at full capacity before attacking. AIs also consider nearby threats before launching.
  • Overpopulation: AI planets with 160+ ships have a 10% chance every 10 seconds to launch at the weakest enemy planet.
  • Dominant Player Targeting 🎯: Players exceeding 4,500 total capacity, leading by 200+ Tech Score, or having 2× capacity of 2nd place are marked — all AIs prioritize attacking them!
  • Homeworld Priority: AIs have a 75% chance to prioritize recapturing their lost homeworld over other targets.
  • Rampage Events: Every ~15 minutes (accelerating to min 6 minutes), a dormant AI awakens on a neutral planet. It targets the smallest neutral with >150 cap (or largest overall). It gets +300% bonus ships and produces at 3× rate. Rampage planets slowly decay in capacity until captured.
  • Fog of War: Optional. You can only see within your gravity wells and ship radar. Previously discovered planets appear as silhouettes.
  • AFK Protection: After 3 minutes of inactivity, your empire is handed to an AI.
  • Map Layout: Default 60 planets. Sizes: Mega-Super (300 cap), Super (250), Large (150–180), Medium (120–150), Small (75–115), Tiny (53–74). Huge maps get more super planets.
  • Configurable Settings: AI count, fog of war, production speed (0.5×–3×), hazard level (0–4×), map size (800–3200), and planet count — all set from the start screen.
  • Leaderboard: Shows players ranked by total capacity. 🧪 = √Tech bonus %. 🎯 = √Exp×0.5 bonus %. 🪐 = capacity (% of galaxy).
  • Player Name: Your name is saved locally and remembered between sessions.
